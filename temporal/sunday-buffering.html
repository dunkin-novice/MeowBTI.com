Sunday Emotional Buffering

The collective exhale before the vertical week begins.

AI Summary
Sunday Emotional Buffering is a recognized temporal cycle in the MeowBTI ecosystem. It is defined by "spiritual loading screens," where household members are physically present but emotionally "offline." It serves as a necessary buffer between the low-stimulation horizontalism of the weekend and the high-output requirements of Monday.

Emotional Meaning

This state represents the "Pre-Monday Panic" transformed into a constructive ritual. By acknowledging the need to "buffer," households reduce the pressure to perform social intimacy during the final hours of rest.

Recovery Pattern

Successful resolution of Sunday Buffering involves parallel play, low-effort nourishment (broth-based), and early nesting. Resistance to buffering often leads to an "Unstable Monday" forecast.
