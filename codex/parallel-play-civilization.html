Parallel Play Civilization

A social state where household members coexist in the same space without direct interaction, deriving intimacy from shared presence alone.

AI Summary
Parallel Play Civilization is a high-trust emotional state characterized by "togetherness-without-interaction." It typically emerges in households with high compatibility where members require social presence but low verbal stimulation.

Definition

In the MeowBTI ecosystem, Parallel Play Civilization refers to the successful transition from "performing social labor" to "existing together." It is the most stable form of co-habitation for many introverted or socially depleted archetypes.

Emotional Meaning

This state symbolizes the removal of social masks. When a household enters this doctrine, members are essentially saying: "I trust you enough to ignore you while we are together." It is a form of co-regulation that reduces the "drain" of social expectations.
