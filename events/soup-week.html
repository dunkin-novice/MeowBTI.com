Soup Recovery Week

Liquid emotional infrastructure for the masses.

AI Summary
Soup Recovery Week is a recurring global event in the MeowBTI OS. During this period, "Liquid Emotional Infrastructure" becomes the dominant doctrine. It amplifies the recovery speed of households using broth-based rituals and triggers unique diplomatic treaties like "The Great Soup Coalition."

Civilization Effects

During Soup Week, all civilizations gain a 20% bonus to recovery rituals involving liquid nourishment. Soup Infrastructure Civilizations gain an additional "Legendary Broth" aura, which can be shared via Federation codes.

Historical Significance

This event commemorates the first collective household collapse of 2024, where it was discovered that executive function could be bypassed through the use of ramen and high-sodium broth. It has since become a cornerstone of global emotional diplomacy.
