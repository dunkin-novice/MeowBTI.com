Weaponized Soup Dependency

Warning: Rogue doctrine detected in adjacent systems.

Ecosystem AI Summary
Weaponized Soup Dependency is an unstable mutation of standard Soup-Based Recovery. Civilizations afflicted by this rogue doctrine use the preparation or ordering of liquid nourishment as a passive-aggressive shield against necessary conflict resolution, leading to a toxic buildup of unspoken tension despite adequate hydration.

Incident Timeline

Day 1: Excessive Ramen Delivery
Day 2: Refusal to Chew Solids
Day 3: Broth-Based Diplomacy Fails

Civilization Impact

Households exposed to this anomaly often experience a sharp decrease in verbal communication. Rituals become purely transactional. If left unchecked, the civilization may fragment into a Parallel Play Schism.
