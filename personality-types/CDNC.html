Verdict Summary / สรุปความป่วน

The Joyful Performer (CDNC) lives for the spotlight. Commanding and casual, they turn every mundane activity into a theatrical performance. They are the 'ตัวมารดาด้านการละคร' who demands an audience for their every jump and meow. Life with them is a constant TikTok trend.
