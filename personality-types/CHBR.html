CHBR: The Grand General

"Everything in this house is a resource, and I am the manager. Your compliance is expected."

The Feline Authority Breakdown

This is the rare, powerful cat who operates with the cold efficiency of a corporate executive. The Grand General is **Commanding** because they seek external engagement, **Hunter** because they rely on observable facts, **Bossy** because they prioritize logic over emotion, and **Regal** because they demand structure and closure. They aren't trying to be mean; they're just running a tight ship.
