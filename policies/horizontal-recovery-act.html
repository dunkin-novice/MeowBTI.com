Horizontal Recovery Act

ENACTED. Emotional Infrastructure Protected.

Policy Summary
The Horizontal Recovery Act is an emotional governance policy typically passed via household referendum during periods of severe burnout. Civilizations that enact this policy legally protect horizontal rest, making "doing nothing" an officially recognized form of infrastructure maintenance. It increases civilizational stability but may lead to an "Infrastructure Obsessed" trait.

Civilization Impact

Households that pass this act often see an immediate reduction in internal friction. Rituals like Blanket Deployment become mandatory, and the civilization alignment drifts toward Stable or Recovery-Oriented.

Historical Consequences

While highly effective at diffusing crises, prolonged enforcement of this act can lead to the Parallelized Society trait, where household members become too comfortable in their respective horizontal zones to re-engage in vertical communication.
