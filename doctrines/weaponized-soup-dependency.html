Weaponized Soup Dependency

RESTRICTED. Diplomatic Embargo Active.

Sanction Summary
Weaponized Soup Dependency is an unstable mutation of standard Soup-Based Recovery. Civilizations afflicted by this rogue doctrine use the preparation or ordering of liquid nourishment as a passive-aggressive shield against necessary conflict resolution, leading to a toxic buildup of unspoken tension. It is currently under a Federation-wide embargo.

Underground Activity

Despite the embargo, this doctrine continues to spread through illicit networks, primarily coordinated by the Basement Soup Collective. Law enforcement notes a sharp rise in contraband Instant Ramen Scrolls circulating across Federation borders.

Civilization Impact

Households exposed to this anomaly often experience a sharp decrease in verbal communication. Rituals become purely transactional. If left unchecked, the civilization may fragment into a Parallel Play Schism and experience complete treaty dissolution.
