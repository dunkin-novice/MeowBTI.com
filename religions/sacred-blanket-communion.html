The Sacred Blanket Communion

"Blessed are those who remain horizontal."

Theological Summary
The Sacred Blanket Communion is an advanced evolutionary state of Blanket-Based Governance. Civilizations adhering to this faith view rest not as a biological necessity, but as a moral imperative. They frequently canonize artifacts associated with warmth and often experience ideological schisms over the "purity" of their recovery methods.

Major Schisms

The most notable ideological conflict within this religion is The Blanket Purity Divide. Orthodox practitioners (Quiet Orthodoxy) believe in solitary, undisturbed rest, while extremists (Radical Rot Theory) argue that productive tasks can be performed horizontally without violating the sanctity of the blanket.
