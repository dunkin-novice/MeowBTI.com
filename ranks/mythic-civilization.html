Mythic Civilization

The threshold where survival becomes legend.

AI Summary
A Mythic Civilization (Tier 5) is achieved when a household reaches 100+ Prestige points. This typically requires 60+ days of emotional history, the forging of 10+ relics, and the generation of multiple historical chronicles. It signifies a household that has successfully navigated multiple "Loud Sagas" and stabilized its internal culture.

Philosophy

At the Mythic level, the household is no longer just a group of individuals; it is a storied institution. The artifacts on the shelf are not just objects; they are witnesses to shared survival. The "Inside Jokes" have matured into "Cultural Canon."

Unlock Conditions

100+ Prestige Points
60 Days of History
10+ Forged Relics
3+ Alliances signed
